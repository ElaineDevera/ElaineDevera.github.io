About The Artist

Elaine is from Milpitas, California and is currently a studio art practice major at SJSU. She enjoys drawing, especially when it comes to digital art. She has enjoyed doing art since she was in elementary school and has continued drawing since then. She aspires to be a graphic designer or a gaming illustrator.
